The Experience

A calm start, then the rush.

Every trip begins on the riverbank with a full safety briefing and a paddle lesson. No experience needed — just bring a swimsuit and a sense of adventure.

Briefing & gear

Helmet, life vest and wetsuit fitted on the bank. We don't push off until everyone's confident.

Guided every metre

A licensed river guide rides in every raft, reading the water and calling each paddle stroke.

Photos on us

Our guide shoots the whole run on a waterproof camera. You get the full gallery the same day, free.

"Read the river, trust the team."

Where you'll paddle

The green heart of Lori

Lori is Armenia's forested north — deep river gorges, medieval monasteries on the cliffs, and the cleanest snowmelt water in the country. Our base sits a short drive from Vanadzor, Alaverdi and Stepanavan.
